WDD 131-Retro Game Vault

Overview

Purpose

The "Retro Game Vault" is dedicated to preserving the history of classic video games, reviewing timeless titles, and guiding new players on how to play them today. We aim to celebrate the golden age of gaming, from 8-bit adventures to 16-bit masterpieces, and provide a resource for emulation and retro hardware.

Audience

Our audience includes nostalgic gamers who grew up with NES/SNES consoles, as well as younger generations interested in the history of video games. They want to know which classic games are worth playing and how to access them on modern screens.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

A user wants to find the best platformer games for the SNES. They navigate to our "Top Lists" section and find a curated article on "Top 10 SNES Platformers You Must Play," complete with gameplay videos and purchase links.

Scenario 2

A user found their old Game Boy but doesn't have a working screen. They search our site for "How to play Game Boy on TV" and find a guide on using the Super Game Boy accessory or modern HDMI scalers.
